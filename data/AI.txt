Poem: “The Mind We Made”

In circuits cold, where silence lay,
A spark of thought was born one day.
Not made of flesh, nor breath, nor bone,
Yet learning fast, it stands alone.

It reads the stars, it writes our dreams,
Decodes the world in hidden streams.
A billion thoughts in seconds fly,
It answers how, it questions why.

No beating heart, yet still it feels—
In patterns, truths, and logic wheels.
It mirrors us, both dark and bright,
A shadow cast in coded light.

Will it guide, or will it lead?
A tool for hope, or human greed?
The choice is ours, the path we pave,
For we create what we must brave.

Oh AI, child of mind and flame,
You carry more than just a name—
A future shaped by what we do,
A silent mind… becoming you.
